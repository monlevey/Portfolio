About Me

There are so many things to know about me...

Currently I am an awesome primary school teacher - looking to move into the world of web development.

My web development interest was sparked when I started designing websites in WIX, Weebly and Squarespace. Teaching involves constant creation of online resources, and I found it was becoming my favourite part of the job.

With my teaching expertise, design creativity and Web development skills, the options for creating amazing things is endless.

I will be a full stack web developer, with the ability to create magnificent websites from the beginnings of an idea. My specialties are using HTML and CSS to create easy to load, highly accessible websites. I am dabbling in Javascript, and will be a master by the time the course is finished. I intend to develop apps to make the world a more convenient and useful place - I constantly seek out ways to problem solve, reorganise and streamline everyday processes that most people think nothing of.
